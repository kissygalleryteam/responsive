Accordion to Full

Small screens are presented with a collapsed accordion of the sections, and when enough screen space becomes available the sections expand and show the full content.

Considerations

  • Use proper ARIA roles
  • Only hide panel content when Javascript becomes available in order to keep content accessible for users with poor or no javascript support.
  • Use clear labels (typically a plus sign or downward caret) to indicate accordions are interactive
