Fluid Search Form

A fully-fluid search form.

Considerations

  • Remember, a field placeholder is not a substitute for a form label.
  • Utilize box-sizing: border-box; to create fully fluid form fields with fixed amounts of padding.
  • Use type=search to pull up the search-specific virtual keyboard on many touch devices
